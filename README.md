## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this like hiring a fractional CTO?**  
A: Yes, but focused specifically on open source infrastructure and operations rather than product development or team building. I handle your self-hosted stack so you can focus on your core business.

**Q: What if I need more than 15 hours in a month?**  
A: Additional hours are billed at $250/hour. Most clients stay within the 15-hour limit after the first 1-2 months once core infrastructure is established.

**Q: Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?**  
A: No. I handle all the technical work. You just need to provide access to your systems and make business decisions about which tools to use and when to migrate.

**Q: Where does the software run?**  
A: On your infrastructure—AWS, DigitalOcean, Hetzner, Google Cloud, or any provider you prefer. You maintain full control and ownership. I can also advise on the most cost-effective hosting options.

**Q: What happens if something breaks?**  
A: Business hours support with 4-hour response time is included. For mission-critical systems, after-hours emergency support is available for an additional $500/month.

**Q: Can you migrate our existing data?**  
A: Yes, data migration is a core part of every tool deployment. I ensure zero data loss and minimal downtime during migrations.

**Q: How long does a typical migration take?**  
A: Simple tools (analytics, monitoring): 1-3 days  
Medium complexity (CRM, support): 1-2 weeks  
Complex systems (ERP, custom integrations): 3-4 weeks

**Q: Do you sign NDAs and work within compliance requirements?**  
A: Yes. I regularly work under NDAs and can accommodate security, compliance, and regulatory requirements (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, etc.).

**Q: I'm technical. Can't I just do this myself?**  
A: Absolutely! That's why I built nocodo. But most technical founders tell me: "I *could* do this, but I should be building product instead." It's a matter of highest-value use of your time.

**Q: Why 3-month minimum?**  
A: Real infrastructure changes take time. The 3-month commitment ensures we can deliver meaningful results and complete at least 2-3 significant migrations. Most clients continue beyond 3 months because the value is clear.

**Q: Do you work with companies outside India?**  
A: Yes! I work with companies globally. My async-first approach and flexible hours work across any timezone. Most of my clients are in the US and Europe.

**Q: Can you help with just one migration without the monthly retainer?**  
A: Yes, one-time migrations start at $3,500 per tool. However, most companies find the monthly retainer more cost-effective since you'll typically want to migrate 3+ tools over time.
